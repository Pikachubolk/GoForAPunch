OP - Go For a Punch

"Some weird anime about naked girls stuck in a large bathroom without doors, having a philosophical debate about never getting out of the room. After losing hope and starving for some days, everything goes downhill. They just start screaming and yelling. Then they all commit suicide, either by smashing their heads on the floor or scratching their necks to bloody pieces."

Saki Sanobashi is a supposedly lost horror anime about the unsettling experiences of girls trapped in a confined setting. Since it was first mentioned in a forum post in 2015, various users have reported witnessing something similar, sparking a quest to uncover this alleged lost anime.

History

On February 21st, 2015, a user of the /x/ (Paranormal) board on 4chan described watching an unusual and violent anime referred to as "Go for a Punch".

Another user on the /a/ (Anime) board inquired about it and was informed that it might be titled Saki Sanobashi. This name gained popularity among those interested in the mystery anime.

Spread

Over the next few years, fans of the story embarked on a quest to find the elusive anime. However, the search proved to be challenging as some individuals intentionally introduced misinformation and false leads, complicating the investigation.
